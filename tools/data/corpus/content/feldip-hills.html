The Feldip Hills are positioned to the south of Yanille and south-east of the Castle Wars arena. The main city of Feldip Hills is Gu'Tanoth—to the west of Gu'Tanoth is Jiggig. Jiggig is filled with zogres and skogres. In the southern areas is a Hunter training area. After completing the quest Big Chompy Bird Hunting, the hunting area can be used to hunt chompy. After speaking to Haris with Dragon Slayer I completed, the Corsair Cove Resource Area in the south-west of Feldip Hills can be unlocked and used as a spot to harpoon fish for tuna and swordfish, cage fish for lobster, or cut yew and maple trees.

Transportation

[edit | edit source]

The Feldip Hills can be accessed using a variety of ways including:

  • Feldip hills teleport
  • Gnome glider, (Lemantolly Undri) unlocked after completing One Small Favour.
  • The ogre boat ride, unlocked after completing Freeing Skrach Uglogwee.
  • Fairy ring AKS is near the Feldip Hunter area.
  • Eagle transport system via jungle eagle.
  • Travel to Yanille (using, for example, a Yanille teleport tablet or Watchtower Teleport or the Minigame Teleport to Nightmare Zone) and then walk south.
  • Travel to Castle Wars (using, for example, a ring of dueling) and then walk south and east.
  • Hunter skillcape
  • Spirit tree

Quests

[edit | edit source]

The Feldip Hills has three quest start points which are:

  • Big Chompy Bird Hunting
  • Zogre Flesh Eaters
  • Dragon Slayer II

It is also involved with:

  • Recipe for Disaster - Freeing Skrach Uglogwee
  • Watchtower
  • One Small Favour
  • The Corsair Curse
  • Mourning's End Part I

Hunter

[edit | edit source]
Main article: Feldip Hunter area

The Feldip Hills hunting area is the only hunting area entitled "jungle". It is home to various Hunter creatures and the Hunting expert is located here, who is able to give you the Hunter skillcape when level 99 Hunter is achieved.

  • Tracking: Feldip weasels
  • Bird snares: Crimson swifts and Tropical wagtails
  • Butterflies: Black warlocks
  • Deadfall trapping: Barb-tailed kebbits
  • Box trapping: Carnivorous chinchompas
  • Pitfalls: Spined larupias

Trivia

[edit | edit source]
  • The name is a reference to the Mendip Hills, an area of limestone hills situated to the south of Bristol and Bath in Somerset, England. In RuneScape Classic, the Watchtower Wizard actually calls the region "the mendips".
