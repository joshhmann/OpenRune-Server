Protection prayers

Protection Prayers are often used to refer to the three "Protect from x" Prayers that could nullify or mitigate damage from incoming attacks of the respective combat style or discipline from monsters or players. These are available in both free-to-play and pay-to-play.

Level Prayer Effect Drain Rate Drain Effect
37 Protect from Magic Protects against magic attacks 1 point per 3 seconds 12
40 Protect from Missiles Protects against ranged attacks
43 Protect from Melee Protects against melee attacks

Only one protection prayer can be activated at a time; activating a different protection Prayer will deactivate the current one. Protection Prayers only provide a 40% damage reduction in PVP. Protection prayers block all damage in most circumstances against NPCs, though some bosses (typically high-levelled) have attacks that can deal partial or full damage through them.

When an attack fully ignores protection prayers, it's known as being "typeless". Most typeless attacks are used by NPCs, though there are some that can be used by the player.

Several monsters are able to make use of protection prayers as well.

Trivia

[edit | edit source]
  • The Kalphite Queen, in 2004, was the first monster to use simultaneous protection prayers, with her first form using Protect from Magic and Missiles simultaneously. However, this did not grant her true immunity, instead only representing her increased defence against those styles.
    • The Judge of Yama, encountered during A Kingdom Divided in 2021, was the first monster to use true full-immunity simultaneous prayers. This reflected a shift in Jagex' design philosophy, as similar prayers became a key mechanic in future high-level combat encounters.
