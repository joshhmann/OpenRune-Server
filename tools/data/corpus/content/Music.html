Music in Old School RuneScape is comprised largely of in-game tracks tied to specific locations or quests. These compositions draw inspiration from a wide variety of musical genres, and can be unlocked for playback using the in-game Music Player. A counter at the bottom of the Music Player screen tracks how many pieces of music have been unlocked and how many are yet to be unlocked.

Players who unlock all non-holiday music tracks will be able to purchase the music cape from Olaf the Bard, and subsequently unlock the Air Guitar emote. Unlocking holiday music tracks is required to trim the music cape. Holiday event music from previous years will be unlocked when a player completes the current year's event for that specific holiday.

As of 20 May 2026, there are a total of 839 music tracks (872 when including unlisted and unused music, and 1174 when including Jingles) found in Old School RuneScape.

On 6 March 2007, RuneScape's existing music tracks were updated as part of an audio rework; Old School uses these reworked tracks. The original versions can be found under the "Versions" section of articles for music tracks released prior to March 2007.

Updated tracks

[edit | edit source]

The following tracks have been updated since Old School RuneScape was released:

  • On 26 February 2015, the original unused version of The Trade Parade was replaced with the present version using updated instruments.
  • On 7 June 2016, the unlisted track Massacre was removed from the game.
  • On 7 June 2018, Clan Wars was updated as it was incorrectly using pianos instead of drums.
  • On 26 September 2019, the unused tracks Game Intro 1, Ambience 2, Ambience 3, Ambience 4, and Fade Test were removed from the game.
  • On 23 April 2020, Predator Xarpus, Arachnids of Vampyrium and Dance of the Nylocas were all reworked.
  • On 4 June 2020, Conspiracy was extended in length while Distant Land was slightly changed.
