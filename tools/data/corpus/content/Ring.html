Ring
For all items and their stats worn in this slot, see Ring slot table.

A ring is a piece of jewellery worn in the ring slot of the Worn Equipment tab.

Crafting rings

[edit | edit source]

If the player has the requisite Crafting skill level, these rings can be created by using a ring mould, either a gold bar or a silver bar, and a gem (when applicable) on a furnace.

Most rings can be enchanted using Magic, adding teleportation or other unique effects. Enchanted rings are only available for members, except the ring of forging, which can be obtained in free-to-play by paying 250 coins to Murky Matt in the south-east quadrant of the Grand Exchange.

Other rings

[edit | edit source]

The Hallowed ring negates all direct damage within the Hallowed Sepulchre. The Elven signet provides a 10% chance to not use charges of crystal equipment. The Explorer's ring can be used to teleport to the cabbage patch south of Falador. The Ring of endurance extends the effects of stamina potions and passively reduces run energy drain by 15% when charged with 500 or more doses of stamina potion. The Celestial ring gives an invisible +4 boost to Mining and also gives a chance of obtaining an extra ore up to adamantite. The Lightbearer regenerates the player's special attack energy twice as fast as normal.

Imbued rings

[edit | edit source]

Some rings may be imbued with an enchantment as a reward from one of several combat minigames, doubling their combat bonuses. Options to imbue the rings include Nightmare Zone points from the Nightmare Zone, Zeal Tokens from Soul Wars, or scrolls of imbuing from the Emir's Arena.
