Attack is a player's accuracy in melee combat. As a player raises their Attack level, they can deal damage more consistently as well as wield weapons of stronger materials.

Fighting accurately

[edit | edit source]

In order to efficiently gain experience in the Attack skill, players must choose either the accurate or controlled attack style. While using the accurate style, players gain an invisible +3 attack bonus and gain 4 Attack experience per point of damage inflicted. While using the controlled style, players gain an invisible +1 attack bonus (and +1 strength and +1 defence bonus) and gain 1.33 Attack experience per point of damage inflicted. Attack is used to calculate the accuracy of your attacks against an enemy's defence.

Attack bonuses

[edit | edit source]

Some weapons give players Attack bonuses when using certain attack types. These bonuses are noted in the Equipment Stats section of the equipment menu. Attack bonuses give players advantages as well as disadvantages in melee combat, based on their target's strengths and weaknesses.

Attack type Suitable weapons
Stab Daggers, swords, longswords, and pickaxes.
Slash Swords, longswords, axes, scimitars, battleaxes, and two-handed swords.
Crush Maces, battleaxes, warhammers, pickaxes, axes, and two-handed swords.

Other Attack bonuses

[edit | edit source]
  • Head slot
    • A Dwarven helmet gives +6 crush attack bonus.
    • A Zombie helmet gives +8 crush attack bonus.
  • Neck slot
    • The beads of the dead gives +1 to all melee attack bonuses.
    • An amulet of accuracy gives +4 to all melee attack bonuses.
    • An amulet of power gives +6 to all melee attack bonuses.
    • An amulet of glory gives +10 to all melee attack bonuses.
    • An amulet of fury gives +10 to all melee attack bonuses, but greater Defence, Strength, and Prayer bonuses than the amulet of glory.
    • An amulet of torture gives +15 to all melee attack bonuses.
    • The amulet of rancour gives +25 to all melee attack bonuses.
  • Cape slot
    • The Mythical cape gives +6 crush attack bonus.
    • The Fire cape gives +1 to all melee attack bonuses.
    • The Infernal cape gives +4 to all melee attack bonuses.
  • Ring slot
    • A Warrior ring (i) gives +8 slash attack bonus.
    • A Bellator ring gives +20 slash attack bonus.
    • A Brimstone ring gives +4 to all melee attack bonuses.
  • Shield slot
    • The Unholy book gives +8 to all melee attack bonuses.
    • The Antler Guard gives +15 to all melee attack bonuses.
    • A Dragon defender gives +25 stab, +24 slash, and +23 crush attack bonuses.
    • The Avernic defender gives +30 stab, +29 slash, and +28 crush attack bonuses.
  • Hands slot
    • Any gloves received as a reward from the Culinaromancer's Chest as a reward from the Recipe for Disaster quest gives substantial attack bonuses in stab, slash, and crush; with later unlocks having greater bonuses.
      • Bronze gloves give +2 to all melee attack bonuses.
      • Iron gloves give +3 to all melee attack bonuses.
      • Steel gloves give +4 to all melee attack bonuses.
      • Black gloves give +5 to all melee attack bonuses.
      • Mithril gloves give +6 to all melee attack bonuses.
      • Adamant gloves give +7 to all melee attack bonuses.
      • Rune gloves give +8 to all melee attack bonuses.
      • Dragon gloves give +9 to all melee attack bonuses.
      • Barrows gloves give +12 to all melee attack bonuses.
    • The Ferocious gloves give +16 to all melee attack bonuses.

See also the highest possible bonuses for each style.
