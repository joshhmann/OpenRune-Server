Books are items obtained in quests and elsewhere in Old School RuneScape. Most books can be read by players, which provides information ranging from lore to information on game mechanics.

When a player reads a book, an interface will appear to display the book's contents, and their character will be seen reading a green (sometimes yellow) book.

Books in bookcase

[edit | edit source]

Bookcases are built in the Parlour, Quest Hall or Study of a player-owned house. All of the bookcases will contain the same books. Bookcases have the ability to save bank space, because they automatically store most books from previously completed quests and other miscellaneous books.

Inventory-only books

[edit | edit source]

Having these books in your inventory, and using them on the bookcase gives this message: There doesn't seem to be space for that on the bookcase.

  • God books
  • Chronicle - purchased from Diango's Toy Store
  • Lift manual (Royal Trouble)
  • Mage's book - a reward from Mage Training Arena minigame
  • Smelly journal - obtained from the Fishing Hamlet
  • Arceuus Library texts (excluding Varlamore envoy)
  • From the town of Slepe:
    • Bloody grimoire
    • Buried alive
    • Deed
    • Explosive discovery
    • The turncloak
    • Elixir of everlasting
  • From Wise Old Man's bookshelves:
    • Book on chickens
    • Book of folklore
    • Strange book
  • Kharedst's memoirs - a reward from Client of Kourend quest
  • Book of the dead - a reward from A Kingdom Divided quest
  • Master scroll book - a possible reward from opening reward caskets
  • Slashed book - After the Elemental Workshop I quest, it will be found in the same house south of the Elemental Workshop in Seers' Village. The Battered book, however, can be found within the bookcase; use a knife on the Battered book to get the Slashed book. However, either book can be used in the Elemental Workshop to make an Elemental shield or Mind shield.
  • Books given as rewards:
    • Tome of experience - a reward from Darkness of Hallowvale quest. After the third reading the book "crumbles into a dust cloud of knowledge."
    • Book of knowledge - a reward from the Surprise Exam random event
    • Duradel's notes - a reward from Kuradal after While Guthix Sleeps quest

    • How to become the Ultimate Wizard of the Universe
    • 101 Ways to Impress Your Mates with Magic
