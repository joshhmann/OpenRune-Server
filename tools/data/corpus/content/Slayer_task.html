A Slayer assignment is given by a Slayer master. Killing the assigned monsters grants players Slayer experience.

For some monsters, players may also be able to access more exclusive locations when assigned them as a task. These monsters can only be killed as part of a slayer assignment; attempting to kill them without the required task will prevent the player from doing so.

Some monsters can only be killed using the Slayer skill. For a list of these monsters, see Slayer monsters.

Task weighting

[edit | edit source]

Task weighting is the system that Slayer masters use to assign Slayer assignments. Each possible assignment has a weight value — the higher this value, the more likely that task will be assigned. The percentage chance of getting assigned a given Slayer task can be calculated using the formula

(wS×100)%

where w is the task's weight and S is the sum of all weights for the particular Slayer master that the player has the requirements to be assigned. This means that the weights of blocked tasks, tasks that are toggled off, and tasks that the player does not have the Slayer level or quest requirements for are not added to the sum S.

For example, below is a hypothetical list of monsters that a Slayer master may assign:

Monster Task weight
Spiritual creatures 8
Dark beast 10
TzHaar 9
Abyssal demon 10
Aviansie 6
Metal dragons 14

If a player has level 85 Slayer, and has not unlocked TzHaar and Aviansie tasks via Slayer reward points, then the list will be filtered so that the player can only be assigned abyssal demons, spiritual creatures, and metal dragons. The player cannot boost their Slayer level to obtain a task above their base Slayer level; eating a wild pie to boost to 90 Slayer (which means dark beasts can be attacked) will not make that task available.

In this case, the total weight would be 32. The player would have a 31.25% chance of being assigned abyssal demons, 25% chance for spiritual creatures and a 43.75% chance of being assigned metal dragons.

It is now possible to unlock the chance to receive a Lizardmen slayer task from Duradel, Nieve and Chaeldar. Unlocking this task costs 75 Slayer points.

(Update)
  • Gargoyles, nechryaels, abyssal demons, spiritual mages, spiritual rangers and spiritual warriors can now be assigned by Vannaka.
  • Chaeldar will now assign between 110 and 170 spirit creatures and aviansie.
  • Removed a number of terrible assignments from Duradel.
  • Duradel will now assign between 130 and 200 nechryaels, spiritual mages and spiritual rangers.
  • Nieve can now assign between 10 and 20 black dragons.
  • Nieve can now assign dark beasts.

(Update)

Players who click out of the dialogue by accident when being offered a Jad slayer task now have 1 minute where they can talk to the master and be re-offered this choice.

16 April 2015
(Update)

Vultures now count toward a birds slayer task.

5 March 2015
(Update | poll)

You now receive 10% more Slayer experience when killing a creature on task in the Slayer Tower if you have completed the Morytania Elite Diary.
