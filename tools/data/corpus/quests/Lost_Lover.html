Lost Lover is an unnamed miniquest not shown in the quest list. It is started by talking to either Fabia or Xilo in the Tlati Rainforest. There are no rewards for completing the miniquest.

Walkthrough

[edit | edit source]

Starting Out

[edit | edit source]

Speak to either Fabia or Xilo in the Tlati Rainforest.

  • Fabia can be found by the southern magic trees west of Kastori. She claims she agreed to meet Xilo by the "magic tree," but Xilo has not arrived.
  • Xilo can be found by the river bank west of the Crypt of Tonali lift platform. She claims she agreed to meet Fabia by the "special tree," but Fabia has not arrived.

Finishing up

[edit | edit source]

Speak to either Fabia or Xilo, whoever you did not speak to first. One will leave their current location to join the other.
