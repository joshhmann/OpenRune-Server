Prince Ali Rescue (#10)
Released 28 February 2001 (Update)
Members No
Quest series Kharidian, #1
Lead developer(s) Rob L, Paul Gower

Prince Ali Rescue is a free-to-play quest, and one of the first released. It's notable for allowing entry through the Al Kharid/Lumbridge gate without the fee of 10 coins. It also introduces characters who are a part of the Kharidian quest series.

Walkthrough

[edit | edit source]

Setting up (obtaining the required items)

[edit | edit source]
Items needed: axe, tinderbox, pickaxe, shears, 16 coins

Skip this part if you have all the required items (can be bought on the Grand Exchange).

  • Purchase the pink skirt at Thessalia's Fine Clothes immediately south of the Varrock town square.
  • Buy 3 beers at the Blue Moon Inn just south-east of Thessalia's Fine Clothes, and pick up the pot in the bar.
  • Cut down a tree, light the logs, then wait for it to burn out to take the ashes.
  • Head to the south-east Varrock mine and mine one tin and one copper ore to later make a bronze bar, and pick one bunch of redberries from the nearby bushes.
  • Head to the south-west Varrock mine, and mine one clay.
  • Head to Fred the Farmer's pen north of Lumbridge and shear three sheep for wool. Pick two onions from the garden behind his house.
  • Pick some wheat from the nearby grain field, then make a pot of flour at the windmill.
  • Spin the wool on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the Lumbridge Castle using the spinning wheel to get three balls of wool.
  • Take the jug from the castle's kitchen basement and fill it with water, then use it on your clay to create soft clay, and then refill it.
  • Head to Al Kharid through the toll gate to the east across the river with your 10 coins, and smith a bronze bar on the furnace there.

The remaining items can be gathered during the quest.

Planning a rescue

[edit | edit source]
Items needed: 3 balls of wool, rope (unless buying from Ned), 2 onions or yellow dye, 1 redberries, pot of flour, ashes, a bucket of water or jug of water, soft clay, a bronze bar, 3 normal beers, a pink skirt, and 30 coins (unless teleporting)
Leela's location just east of Draynor Village.

Head to the Al Kharid palace, located in the south of Al Kharid. There is a jug of water in the eastern room if you did not bring one. Talk to Chancellor Hassan in the southern chamber and ask if he needs help; he will direct you towards Spymaster Osman, who can be found outside the northern perimeter of the palace. Osman will explain that Prince Ali, heir to the Emir of Al Kharid, has been taken by a group of bandits to the abandoned jail just east of Draynor Village. He wants you to talk to his daughter Leela, who will help you attempt a rescue.

Leela can be found strolling east of Draynor Village just south of the wheat field. Talk to Leela and ask her about the key and disguise, and she will explain what you will need to pull off the escape.

To the west of Leela is Ned's house. Ask him to make you a wig from the balls of wool. If you haven't brought a rope, you can buy one from his shop for 18 coins (15 through dialogue). Once you have the rope and wig, talk to Aggie in the house just west of Ned's, and ask her to make you some paste. She will use your redberries, ashes, flour and water to make the paste. If you did not bring yellow dye, you can ask her to make it for you in exchange for 2 onions and five coins. Use the dye on the wig to dye it yellow.

Head inside the jail directly east of Draynor, avoiding the aggressive level 26 jail guards. They will ignore you if your combat level is 53 or higher. Once inside, speak to Lady Keli and tell her she's famous, then ask her about her latest plan, then praise her skill. Ask her how she knows someone won't try to free him, and she'll mention the key. Ask her if you can see the key and then if you can touch the key for a short amount of time. You will make a key print of the key using the soft clay. ( 1•1•2•1•1)…

Once you have the key print, head to any furnace to smelt the bronze key using the key print and your bronze bar. The closest is in Lumbridge. Members may use a Amulet of glory to teleport to the Edgeville furnace, and then to teleport to Draynor Village for the next section.

Saving the Prince

[edit | edit source]
Items needed: 3 beers, rope, a pink skirt, a wig (dyed yellow), and paste

Talk to Leela, who will tell you to talk to Joe the guard to find a weakness before breaking the prince out.

Note: If she doesn't tell you to talk to the guard, you may have forgotten to dye the wig yellow.

Talk to Joe and offer him a drink of all three beers, until he is drunk. Use the rope on Lady Keli, the key on the cell door, then talk to Prince Ali and he will use the wig and pink skirt as a disguise and escape.

Return to Hassan in the Al Kharid palace to claim your reward.

Congratulations, quest complete!

Tip: Members may talk to Osman outside the palace after the quest to unlock the Minigame Teleport to the Sorceress's Garden.
