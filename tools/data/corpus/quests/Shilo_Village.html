Shilo Village is a quest in which the player lays to rest a vengeful spirit that is attacking Shilo Village, a town located in southern Karamja. The quest unlocks access to the village and its cart system.

Walkthrough

Map of the Quest

Beginning the quest

[edit | edit source]

Ensure you have one free inventory space, then speak to Mosol Rei outside of Shilo Village, who tells you to run. Ask why, and he will say Rashiliyia has returned. Ask who she is, and he will explain she is the Queen of the dead that has brought a plague of undead upon the village. Ask what you can do, and he will ask you to talk to the shaman Trufitus in Tai Bwo Wannai Village about something called the Legend of Rashiliyia, which may be to blame. Agree to go see the shaman, then agree to take the Wampum belt to Trufitus. ( 1•1•1•4•2)…

1Why do I need to run?
1Rashiliyia? Who is she?
1What can we do?
4I'll go to see the Shaman
2Yes, I'm sure and I'll take the Wampum belt to Trufitus.

Ensure you have the belt in your inventory before continuing! The quest will not officially start until you give it to Trufitus.

The search for the temple of Ah Za Rhoon

[edit | edit source]
Location of Ah Za Rhoon
Items needed: Spade, lit Torch or candle, rope, chisel, 2 free inventory spaces
Items recommended: food and armour or Protect from Melee to run past level 61-73 undead ones

Head to Trufitus at the most north-eastern house in Tai Bwo Wannai and use the wampum belt on him. Ask about the legend, and he will say that any information regarding it was lost in the vanished temple of Ah Za Rhoon. Ask if he knows anything more about the temple, and he will mention that the priest Zadimus built the temple, and it is rumoured that Rashiliyia destroyed it after he rejected her affections. Ask him to tell you more, then tell him you will search for Ah Za Rhoon, and he will be skeptical but agree to help you if you are serious. Say you are serious about looking for Ah Za Rhoon and the quest will officially begin. ( 2•2•1•3•1)…

2Mosol Rei said something about a legend?
2Do you know anything more about the temple?
1Tell me more.
3I am going to search for Ah Za Rhoon!
1Yes, I will seriously look for Ah Za Rhoon and I'd appreciate your help.

The temple of Ah Za Rhoon is located on eastern Karamja, south-west of the Shipyard and east of Shilo Village across the River Amja (the pile of rocks on the minimap). Use a gnome glider or fairy ring DKP, or walk there by crossing the wooden log to the east, then head south until you reach a mound of earth surrounded by rocks.

  • Use your spade on the mound of earth to find a fissure.
  • Use your torch or candle on the fissure to light it up (this consumes the item!).
  • Add a rope to the fissure.
  • Search the fissure and climb down into the dungeon below ( 32 ).
Note: The dirt mound may respawn if you take too long, but you may right click and search it twice to still enter (torch and rope not required).

In the cave are undead ones (level 61 - 73) who may attack you. Killing them occasionally causes a cloud of shilo mist to appear and deal damage to you.

In this cave system, you will need to find four items. Consult the following map to aid in finding them.

1 - Entrance 5 - Waterfall rocks
2 - Cave-in 6 - Loose rocks
3 - Smashed table 7 - Sacks
4 - Strange looking stone 8 - Gallows
  • Start off by heading south until you see the water.
  • Use a chisel on the strange looking stone (4 on the map; it is a small grey stone in the floor to the east of a Smashed chair and south of a stalagmite) to obtain the stone-plaque.
  • Go west past the column and search the Cave in (2 on the map) to crawl through and into some ruins.
  • Head north until you reach the end of the ruined buildings. Search the loose rocks (6 on the map) near the north-west wall to find the tattered scroll. If you fail, you will get hit for 4-7 (possibly more) damage.
  • Now head south-east until you come to a small dead-end branch. Search the sacks next to a smashed table (7 on the map) to obtain the crumpled scroll.
  • Finally, head north to the far end of the cavern, where you will find the ancient gallows (8 on the map). Search it and remove the corpse to obtain the Zadimus corpse.

After acquiring all four items, you can teleport out. Otherwise, to exit the dungeon, return to the smashed table (3 on the map). You can craft the smashed table into a raft and use it to leave. This does not always work; it is possible to get the message that there isn't enough wood left. In this case, you will have to leave by other means. If you want, you can continue south, then east to the end of the cave. Search the rocks you find there to leave via the waterfall. You will take around 20 damage if you fall while leaving this way. You will find yourself near the stepping stones to the east of Shilo Village.

Tomb of Bervirius

[edit | edit source]
Items needed: bronze wire, A chisel, 3 free inventory spaces
Items recommended: food for players with low agility
Skills needed: 32 [boostable] to enter the Tomb of Bervirius

Read both the scrolls and the plaque, then use both scrolls, the plaque, and the corpse on Trufitus. He will tell you about the tomb of Bervirius and instruct you to bury the corpse.

West of the hut is the Tribal Statue, bury the corpse there and the Spirit of Zadimus will appear, giving you a bone shard after some cryptic words. Use the bone shard on Trufitus, and tell him it appeared when you buried the corpse, that the spirit said something, and that it was about keys and kin. He'll tell you to look for Bervirius' tomb. ( 1•1•1)…

1It appeared when I buried Zadimus' corpse
1He said something after he gave it to me.
1The spirit said something about keys and kin?

If you didn't bring bones with you, you can obtain the three required bones from the chickens that spawn inside the village south of Trufitus now for a future section (beware of the poisonous tribesmen).

Head towards Cairn Isle attached to the south-western coast of Karamja by either walking south or teleporting to a fairy ring and using code CKR. Climb the rocks and cross the bridge to reach Cairn Isle. You may fall and take damage, keep trying until you succeed.

Search the well-stacked rocks (i.e. the cairn) on the north side of the island. Enter the crawl-way ( 32 ) to enter the Tomb of Bervirius. You may become stuck many times, but keep trying. Head south to the dolmen, and search it to get a sword pommel, a locating crystal, and Bervirius notes.

Warning: If you drop the sword pommel or the bone beads, they will turn to dust and you will have to go back and get new ones. If you drop the locating crystal, you will take 10 damage.

Note: If you lose the Bervirius notes, you will need charcoal and papyrus to get it again. You can purchase both from the store north of Tai Bwo Wannai.

Read the scrolls. You need to make a new necklace. Use a chisel on the sword pommel to make bone beads. Use the bronze wire on the beads to make the beads of the dead necklace.

You now need to head to eastern Karamja. If walking there, climb up the nearby rockslide to exit the tomb (you may fall and take up to 10 damage). Otherwise you may teleport out to use fairy ring DKP or a gnome glider.

Tomb of Rashiliyia

[edit | edit source]
Items needed: Chisel, bone shard, beads of the dead necklace, locating crystal, 3 regular bones.
Items recommended: Good food (lobsters or higher), weapon, good armour. An emergency teleport for low level players
Skills needed: 20 [boostable] to craft the bone key

Rashiliyia's Tomb is north of the crashed gnome glider, accessible east of Trufitus across the log or south of fairy ring DKP. Look for a searchable palm tree. Avoid the nearby The Shaikahan enemy which cannot be harmed. The entrance is hidden behind a hill and is most visible if you are facing south. Search the palm trees to find two doors. Search these doors to find the shape of the keyhole, and then use your chisel on the bone shard to make a bone key ( 20 ). Put on the beads of the dead necklace before entering or you will be unable to continue and will take constant damage! Use the key on the door to enter.

1 - Entrance 3 - Skeleton door
2 - Rock slope 4 - Dolmen

Once inside, open the ancient gates, then climb down the rocks once you are ready to run past several aggressive undead ones.

Warning: In the next section you may be attacked by undead ones while trying to open the tomb doors by using your bones. The door will remember the amount of bones you have used, so if you die you can return and use the remaining bones.

Head west until you find a stack of coins (do not pick them up or more undead ones will be summoned), then south, and then south-west at the first fork. At the end of this corridor will be a set of doors. Other sets of dummy doors exist if you take a wrong turn, so make sure you're in the right place. Right-click the bones to use them on the door. While still wearing the necklace, enter the room.

Nazastarool

[edit | edit source]

While still wearing the beads of the dead, search the dolmen in the room. You will be attacked by Nazastarool, a level 91 zombie that attacks with Melee. After you defeat him, he will turn into a level 68 skeleton and then a level 93 ghost. You can safespot all three forms by running around the dolmen after each form spawns until he gets stuck (see image). Each form has a 5 minute despawn timer. He has a weakness to Fire spells and Crumble Undead, and is not resistant to any combat style. A salve amulet can be worn once the first form has spawned. A dwarf multicannon may be used.

Note: If you try to leave the cave the way you came in, the door will be locked and Rashiliyia will summon three Undead ones to kill you. You can however still leave by using the bone key on the doors. If you leave and return, you won't need to fight the forms that you already defeated, and you won't need bones to open the tomb door.

Note: A friend may attack the boss for you, but you must be the last person to attack it to progress in the quest (0s count but not poison/venom).

Once you have defeated the ghost, you will get the corpse of Rashiliyia. Teleport out towards Cairn Isle or leave from where you entered (using your key on the door).

Go back to the tomb of Bervirius on Cairn Isle west from Shilo Village, and use Rashiliyia's remains on the dolmen. Rashiliyia will appear before you. She will thank you for letting her rest in peace and give you your reward.

Rewards

[edit | edit source]
  • 2 Quest points
  • 3,875 Crafting experience
  • Access to Shilo Village
  • Ability to quick travel to Shilo Village using the cart near the Brimhaven docks
  • After completing the quest, you can sell every item you obtained during the quest to Yanni Salika in Shilo Village for a total of 2,000 — 100 each for the stone-plaque, tattered scroll, crumpled scroll, Bervirius notes, and the bone key; 500 for the locating crystal; and 1000 for the beads of the dead.
  • Ability to charter the Lady of the Waves west to Shilo Village to Port Khazard or Port Sarim
  • Nazastarool accessible in the Nightmare Zone.
