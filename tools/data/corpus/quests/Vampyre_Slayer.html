Vampyre Slayer is a free-to-play quest that takes place in Draynor Village. The player is asked to slay the terrifying vampyre count Draynor terrorising the village that lives in a large mansion just north of Draynor, with the help of an experienced vampyre slayer.

Walkthrough

[edit | edit source]

The vampyre threat

[edit | edit source]
Morgan's location.

Speak to Morgan in his house in Draynor Village, just north of Ned's house. He will tell you that the village is being ravaged by an evil vampyre that lives in the basement of the nearby manor, and they need someone to get rid of it. He wants you to first talk to the retired vampyre hunter Dr Harlow for tips on how to kill it.

If you don't already have garlic, you can search the cupboard upstairs to find some.


The retired vampyre slayer

[edit | edit source]
Dr Harlow's location at The Blue Moon Inn.

Dr Harlow can be found in the Blue Moon Inn in Varrock, south of the square. Tell him Morgan needs his help, and he will ask for a beer in return, which you can buy from the bartender for 2 coins if you didn't bring one. Talk to him again and he will give you a stake, advise you to use a hammer to drive it in, and to bring some garlic to weaken the vampyre.

If you don't already have a hammer, you can buy one from the Varrock General Store to the north for 1 coin.

Count Draynor

[edit | edit source]
Items needed: weapon, armour, food, garlic, hammer and a stake

Head to Draynor Manor north of Draynor Village, bringing your combat equipment and food, garlic, a hammer and the stake.

Once inside, go down the large stairs on the east side of the building. There are candles in this room that you can walk on, causing the player to exclaim, but these do not deal damage. There is a coffin in the centre of the room. Open it and the vampyre, Count Draynor, will pop up and attack you.

If you brought garlic, upon beginning melee combat, the Count will automatically take 10 damage at the start of the fight, and have his attack/strength reduced by 10 levels, and defence by 40. This effect lasts for 30 seconds.

It is possible to safespot him by running to the south-western corner between the staircase and the skeleton. Once the Count is next to you, step into the tile he is occupying. He should move into the tile you just left, but this may take a couple of tries. Once he is in the corner, move to the square diagonal to him so that you are standing in front of the skeleton. If using the safespot, it is recommended to use Magic as flinching with melee is slow and the Count regenerates health at roughly 6 times the rate of regular monsters.

Upon defeating Count Draynor, you will automatically stab him with the hammer and stake; if you didn't bring a hammer, You are unable to push the stake far enough in will appear in the chat, and you will be unable to kill him.

Note: It is possible to set up a Dwarf Multi Cannon in this area.

Note: If you take too long to kill Count Draynor, he returns to his coffin and heals to full health.

The quest is completed after defeating the count, and you can exit Draynor Manor through the exit in the eastern-most room or teleport.

Congratulations, quest complete!
