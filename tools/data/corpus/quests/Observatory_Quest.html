The Observatory Quest is a quest where you must help the professor of the observatory repair the structure and restore it to its former glory.

Unwelcome neighbours

[edit | edit source]
Items needed: 3 regular planks, a bronze bar, molten glass

Speak with the Observatory professor, located in the reception room of the Observatory near the Observatory tower, north of Castle Wars. Ask him about the Observatory and he will mention that goblins have recently moved in and tampered with its telescope. To repair it, he needs three planks for the tripod, a bronze bar for the tube, and molten glass for a new lens.

Talk to him again three times, and he will take each item from you. Once you give him the final item, he will mention you also need to retrieve a mould for the new lens that has been stolen by goblins. Make sure the professor has taken all of the items before continuing, otherwise you will need to repeat the next section.

Retrieving the lens mould

[edit | edit source]
Items recommended: An antipoison potion, energy potions, combat equipment (for lower levels) and food
Note: If you don't have a form of antipoison, you can pick up a superantipoison at a spawn in the corner of the south-eastern building near Greasycheeks and Smellytoes's fire.

Go down the stairs to the east of the reception room to find the Observatory Dungeon (the room with the stairs is directly east of the start point). Once down the stairs, you must find the goblin kitchen key. It is commonly found in the south-eastern most chest although it can vary from person to person. Search any chests you pass on the way to the south-eastern chest just in case. Once you search the correct chest, complete the dialogue or you won't receive the key.

Note: Sometimes there may be a poisonous spider (level 31) inside the chest.

With the key, follow the north-eastern path west to the kitchen gate. "Prod" the sleeping guard (level 42), who will wake and attack you. Kill him or get him stuck behind a wall and you will be able to pass through the gate.

Note: Getting him stuck will cause him to attack you again when you leave, and another sleeping goblin will spawn.

Open the kitchen gate, consuming the key, and then inspect the goblin stove. You determine the goblins are cooking something foul in the lens mould. Dump out the stew and bring the mould back to the professor.

After speaking to the professor, his assistant will interject and ask you to make the new lens yourself as the professor has a history of explosions when attempting to craft things.

Use the molten glass that the professor just gave you with the lens mould to craft him a new lens. Give it back to the professor. He will want you to visit him in the Observatory.

The Observatory

[edit | edit source]

Climb back down the stairs into the Observatory Dungeon. Now follow the north-west passage (past the kitchen) to find some stairs leading to the Observatory (the southern set of stairs on the map). There is also a chest north-east of the stairs to the Observatory, which spawns infinite Antipoison(1) vials, which you can combine to make full vials.

Go up the stairs, and a cutscene will play. If the cutscene does not play, you may have forgotten to give the finished lens to the professor. After the cutscene ends, go up the stairs and talk to the professor, and then "view" the telescope to see a constellation.

After closing the window, the professor should ask you which constellation you saw. The constellations are on wall charts in the main level of the Observatory. Identify your constellation, talk to the professor again, and you should get your reward in your inventory.

Rewards

[edit | edit source]
  • 2 Quest points
  • 2,250 Crafting experience
  • Your item reward depends on what astrological sign you see when you look through the telescope. It is different for each person. Each one rewards an uncut sapphire as well as one of the following:
    • Aquarius: 25 water runes
    • Aries: 875 Attack experience
    • Cancer: An amulet of defence
    • Capricorn: 875 Strength experience
    • Gemini: A black 2h sword
    • Leo: 875 Hitpoints experience
    • Libra: 3 law runes
    • Pisces: 3 tuna
    • Sagittarius: A maple longbow
    • Scorpio: Weapon poison
    • Taurus: A 1-dose super strength potion
    • Virgo: 875 Defence experience
  • The Spirit of Scorpius will now give you a mould to make unholy symbols, and he will bless them for you too. He can be found in the graveyard north of the Observatory.
  • Talk to the Observatory assistant after the quest and he will reward you with a jug of wine as well as tell you his and the professor's real name.
  • Access to the grappling shortcut rock outside the Observatory.
  • Completing this quest awards the player with 5 Kudos which can be claimed by talking to Historian Minas in the Varrock Museum.

Changes

Date Changes
8 November 2018
(Update)

Constellations that reward combat experience will no longer be assigned to players who are level 1 in the corresponding skill.[3]

Trivia

[edit | edit source]
  • If the player talks to the Observatory assistant after the quest, he will give the player a jug of wine as thanks for the help. He will also tell the player his and the professor's real names, which are Patrick and Mambo-duna-roona, respectively.
  • The professor may give you a chart during or after the quest. Although it is no longer a requirement now, you may still want to keep it for Treasure Trails.
  • After the quest is completed, the drop rate of mithril bolts from chaos druids and elder chaos druids will be slightly decreased, due to the unholy mould drop that is added to their tables.
