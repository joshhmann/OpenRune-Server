Gertrude's Cat is a short quest in which you have to find Fluffs, a lost pet cat owned by Gertrude. Completion of the quest allows you to adopt a feline companion of your very own.

Walkthrough

[edit | edit source]

Starting out

[edit | edit source]
Items needed: 100 coins.

Speak to Gertrude at her house west of Varrock, south of the Cooks' Guild. She has lost her cat, Fluffs, and she wants you to look for her. Her two children were the last to see her. If you don't already have a seasoned sardine, pick up some doogle leaves to the south.

Head to the Varrock Square and talk to Wilough or Shilop. They will tell you about their secret playground, which is the last place they remember seeing Fluffs. Pay them 100 coins to tell you the location, which is the Lumber Yard found north-east of Varrock.

The secret playground (Lumber Yard)

[edit | edit source]
Items needed: Bucket of milk, and seasoned sardine OR raw sardine and doogle leaves.
Items recommended: Lumberyard teleport. An axe to complete an easy Varrock Diary task.

At the Lumber Yard, climb-over the broken fence on the southern side to enter, then climb up the ladder in the centre of the area. There you will find Gertrude's cat, Fluffs. Try to pick-up the cat and she will hiss at you, then use a bucket of milk on her and try to pick her up again. She will still hiss. Use the doogle leaves on the raw sardine to make a seasoned sardine and use it on her. Try to pick her up again and she will be afraid to leave; in the distance you can hear kittens mewing...

Locations of all crates where kittens can potentially be found.

Go down the ladder and search through the nearby crates with the yellow "Mew!" above them. Despite the mews moving around, the location of the kitten remains the same, so keep searching different crates until you find Fluffs' kitten. Click on continue and the kitten appears in your inventory. Do not drop the kitten or it will run away and you will have to find it again.

Note: If the kitten didn't appear in your inventory, log out and back in.


Go back up the ladder and use the kitten on Fluffs, and they will both return home.

Return to Gertrude to complete the quest. Wear a ring of charos (a) if you want to choose the colour of your kitten reward.

Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards

[edit | edit source]
  • 1 Quest point
  • 1,525 Cooking experience
  • Your own kitten
  • Chocolate cake
  • Stew
  • Gertrude will now sell kittens for you to raise for 100 coins each.

Note for ironmen: Keep the chocolate cake quest reward for the A Tail of Two Cats quest and the stew for the The Queen of Thieves quest.

Required for completing

[edit | edit source]

Completion of Gertrude's Cat is required for the following:

  • Icthlarin's Little Helper
  • Recipe for Disaster - Freeing Evil Dave
  • Medium Varrock Diary - Select a colour for your kitten.
