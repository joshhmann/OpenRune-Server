The Blood Moon Rises is an upcoming quest, scheduled for release on 30 June 2026. It will be the sixth and final quest in the Myreque quest series. Completion of the quest will unlock access to Vampyrium, the home realm of the Vampyres.

The Blood Moon Rises was originally announced at RuneFest 2025 and passed a poll in March 2025, with the rewards being polled separately following the Winter Summit in January 2026.

Completion of A Night at the Theatre and Sins of the Father will be required to start the quest.

DescriptionLong ago, before the vampyres were united as one, they lived in disparate clans in their ancestral home of Vampyrium. For the Drakan clan, strength ruled above all else. Clan leaders were selected during a brutal festival of combat known as the Night of the Blood Moon. Along with leadership of the clan, the victor of this festival was granted a title: they became known as the Blood Moon.

Now, thousands of years later, Lord Lowerniel Drakan, the last Blood Moon has returned. He has but one goal: to return his people to the old ways and reclaim his former title, even if he has to sacrifice every life in Morytania to do it.

For Drakan, this is the endgame. Failure is not an option and he does not intend to let anyone stand in his way. No matter what, the Blood Moon will rise once more.

Trivia

[edit | edit source]
  • The RuneScape 3 equivalent to this quest in the Myreque storyline is The Lord of Vampyrium, released in 2015.
    • Unlike the aforementioned quest, this quest will conclude the Myreque storyline. RuneScape 3 concludes the series with a ninth quest, River of Blood, which involves Vanescula Drakan.
  • The Blood Moon Rises may be the second quest released in Old School to complete a quest line from the original game, after Song of the Elves in 2019.
