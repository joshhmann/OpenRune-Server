Pandemonium is a novice quest that introduces players to the Sailing skill. The quest teaches the player how to sail a ship, dock at a port, and carry cargo.

Walkthrough

[edit | edit source]

Job interview

[edit | edit source]

To begin the quest, talk to either Will or Anne on the dock immediately east of the Food Store in Port Sarim. They will eagerly offer you a role within their new salvaging business. When ready, you will be taken aboard their boat, and the pair will reveal that they want to collect some salvage from an old pirate wreck. They will put you in charge of steering the ship; after clicking the helm to control the ship, use the minimap to navigate south, through the Bay of Sarim, to the wreck east of the Karamja Shipyard.

Pirate wreck location, east of Karamja Shipyard.

Upon arriving at the wreck, Anne will ask you to lower the salvaging hook. After deploying the salvaging hook, you'll retrieve a mysterious medallion and an old cup. Will, recognising the medallion, will take it and leave you with the cup. Anne will then shove you overboard.

Pandemonium!

[edit | edit source]

You'll wash up at The Pandemonium. Speak to 'Squawking' Steve Beanie, who will recognise Anne and Will as thieves that stole a map to the wreck, breaking the one rule of the Pandemonium. He'll point you towards Ribs, the map's original owner.

Talk to Ribs in the pub, and he'll share the tale of Crimson Kraken, a deadly vessel captained by the cruel and vicious pirate Barnacle Barnaby. He'll claim that now that Barnaby's medallion has been found, a curse has been unleashed, and will ask you to talk to Steve Beanie again to find Will and Anne. Talk to Steve again, and he'll mention Old Grog, who might be interested in tracking them down. In the meantime, you'll need to acquire your own boat.

Collecting cargo

[edit | edit source]

Head east out of the pub, and talk to Junior Jim on the second docked ship. After giving him the old cup in exchange for a raft, he'll inform you that you need to pick up cargo to get sails for the boat. He'll then take you to the shipyard, where you'll learn how to customize your boat (or in this case, raft).

Pick up a hammer and saw from the two open crates to the south, then board the gangplank. On your raft, build a cargo hold in the facilities hotspot, then use the gangplank to disembark your raft and return to Junior Jim. After giving you a Captain's log, he'll ask you to head over to Port Sarim to collect the cargo.

Use the gangplank to the north east to board your raft, then use the helm to navigate and travel north past the shipyard to Port Sarim. Use the buoy just south of the southernmost dock to dock your raft, then use the gangplank to the north to disembark. Take cargo from the ledger table at the western end of the dock. You must unequip weapons before carrying cargo. Return to the gangplank to board your raft, then open the basic cargo hold to deposit your cargo.

Navigate south back to the docks of The Pandemonium, parking within the perimeter of the buoys. Open the basic cargo hold and withdraw the crate, then use the gangplank to the north west to disembark. Deposit the cargo on the ledger table at the western end of the dock, and talk to Junior Jim to let him know you delivered his cargo. Finally, talk to Steve Beanie in the pub for an update on Will and Anne, and he'll introduce you to Old Grog, who'll agree to help track them down.

Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards

[edit | edit source]
  • 1 Quest point
  • 300 Sailing experience
    • An additional 100 Sailing experience is gained when delivering the cargo, for a total of 400 experience
  • Access to the Sailing skill
  • Access to The Pandemonium
  • A raft
  • 25 sawmill coupon (wood plank)
  • 2 repair kits
  • A spyglass

Trivia

[edit | edit source]
  • Pandemonium was first available in the March 2025 Sailing Alpha and June 2025 Sailing Beta.
  • The Playlist for Will and Anne's boat is the same as the songs played at sea in the Sailing Betas, with the removal of Sea Shanty 2.
  • During the March 2025 Alpha, the quest awarded the 300 Sailing experience it currently gives. However, during the June 2025 Beta it awarded 512 Sailing experience instead.
  • Junior Jim calling the player a "Billy-no-boats" is likely a play on the phrase "Billy no mates".
