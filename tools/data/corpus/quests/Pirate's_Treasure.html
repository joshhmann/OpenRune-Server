Pirate's Treasure is a quest in which you help the pirate Redbeard Frank obtain some Karamjan rum in return for the location of the treasure of One-Eyed Hector, his old captain.

Walkthrough

[edit | edit source]

Captain Redbeard

[edit | edit source]
Items needed: 60 coins.

Talk to Redbeard Frank located in front of the northernmost dock in Port Sarim. Tell him you are in search of treasure, and he will ask you to bring him some Karamjan rum before he'll tell you where to find some.

Walk south on the docks to see three sailors (NOT the Monks of Entrana). Talk to any one of these sailors to board the ship to Karamja for 30 coins (free using a Ring of Charos (a)).

Rum smuggling

[edit | edit source]
Items needed: 30 coins.
Location of the crate used to hide the rum.

When you arrive in Karamja, follow the path to the west and enter the bar. Trade with Zembo and buy one Karamjan rum.

Do not leave the island with the rum - it will be confiscated by the officers as Asgarnia has banned the import of intoxicating spirits. The bottle will also break if you teleport or accept a random event where you would be temporarily teleported.

You will need to find a method to smuggle the rum off the island. First, pick 10 bananas from the plantation to the north. Then talk to Luthas in his hut to the east of the plantation and ask him for a job, and he will hire you as his worker.

Outside the south east corner of his hut is a crate. Use the rum on it to hide it in the crate, then "Fill Crate" on it to fill it with bananas.

Note: If you get a message saying "Why would I want to do that?" make sure you have asked for employment first.

Talk to Luthas again and he will pay you 30 coins for loading the crate. Ask him where the crate goes, and he will tell you they go to Wydin, who owns the food store in Port Sarim. Travel back to Port Sarim at the customs officer to the east for 30 coins (free with Ring of charos (a)).

Retrieving the rum

[edit | edit source]
Location of Hector's chest.
Items recommended: a teleport to Varrock and Falador.

Once you are in Port Sarim, make a quick stop at the fishing shop just to the west. Hanging on the back wall is a white apron. Take it and wear it.

Talk to Wydin at his food store directly south of the fishing shop, and ask him for a job. He will agree as long as you are wearing your white apron. Enter the back room and search the crate with a banana on top of it. You will find the rum. Do not teleport with it in your inventory (including random events). Head back to Redbeard Frank and give him the drink.

Frank will give you a key to the chest found upstairs in Varrock's Blue Moon Inn, owned by the now deceased One-Eyed Hector. Travel to Varrock and head to the Blue Moon Inn located just north of the city's southern gate. Go upstairs and find the chest in the room at the end of the hall. Use the key on the chest to find a pirate message. It will give you a clue to the treasure's location. Be sure to read the clue.

Buried treasure

[edit | edit source]
Items needed: Spade, pirate message
Items recommended: A weapon.

Travel to Falador Park, located in the north of Falador. If you don't yet have a spade, grab one from the estate agency just south-west of the park. Find the statue of Saradomin in the middle of the park, and notice where it's pointing. Note the configuration of the dirt, which vaguely makes an X. Stand in the middle of the dirt "X" and dig. An angry level-4 gardener will appear out of nowhere and attack you. You will be unable to dig again until you either kill him, or run out of the park to despawn him. Dig again in the same spot to get a casket and complete the quest.

Location of the buried treasure.

Congratulations, Quest Complete!

Trivia

[edit | edit source]
  • When using the Casket received as a reward for completing the quest on Redbeard Frank, the player's character offers to share the treasure with him, but he refuses and insists that the player should keep all of it for their own as they have earned it.
  • When the quest was first released, the Karamja rum was tradeable and the bottles would not break if players tried to teleport. Some players simply bought a whole load of rum and sold it to players doing the quest, allowing them to skip a large part of it. When this was posted on fan forums, a Jagex developer saw it and the problem was soon corrected.
  • If you have completed Rum Deal prior to starting this quest, you will ask Redbeard Frank if Braindeath 'Rum' will do instead instead of Karamjan rum, in which case Redbeard refuses immediately, calling it 'swill'.
  • If you give Redbeard Frank some Karamja rum with sliced banana in it, he will take it at first, but will give it back when talked to again, stating that he doesn't like bananas in his rum.
  • If you give Redbeard Frank some Karamja rum with a banana stuffed in it, he will take it at first, but will give it back, saying "Arr - this here rum's got a banana stuck in it!".
  • As the completed quest journal text indicates, it was at one time necessary to complete the quest in order to travel between Port Sarim and Musa Point, as well as to continue to work for Luthas.
