Jungle Potion is a short quest taking place on Karamja. It involves gathering a handful of different herbs for Trufitus Shakaya, in order for him to perform a ritual to communicate with the island's gods.

Walkthrough

[edit | edit source]
Locations of all materials.

An abandoned village

[edit | edit source]

Head to the small village of Tai Bwo Wannai, located south of Brimhaven on Karamja. Talk to Trufitus, found in a small house north-east of the main village area, and ask him where everyone is. He will explain they are afraid to stay in the village. Ask him how you can help, and he will ask for several herbs to mix a potion to commune with the Gods, in order to see what fate befalls them.

Note: You cannot collect all of the herbs at once. You will need to go back to talk with Trufitus each time.

Snake Weed

[edit | edit source]

Head south-west of the village near the ocean to find marshy jungle vine. Search it to get a grimy snake weed; this may take 1-5 minutes depending on your Herblore level. Clean the herb and give it to Trufitus.

Note: If you intend to do Legends' Quest and Zogre Flesh Eaters, take two extra to save yourself time later.

Ardrigal

[edit | edit source]

Head north-east of Trufitus' hut until you reach a peninsula with several palm trees on it, just south of the agility shortcut.

Avoiding the nearby level 46 Harpie Bug Swarms, search a palm tree to find a grimy ardrigal. Clean the herb and give it to Trufitus.

Note: If you intend to do Legends' Quest, take an extra to save yourself time later.

Sito Foil

[edit | edit source]

Warning: The tribesmen in this section are aggressive, and can poison you with damage starting at 11.

Immediately south of the village you will find scorched earth. Search it to find the grimy sito foil. Clean the herb and give it to Trufitus.

Volencia Moss

[edit | edit source]

Head south-east of the hut until you reach a group of rocks you can search that are in the mining area, search an empty one to find the grimy volencia moss. Clean the herb and give it to Trufitus.

Note: If you intend to do Fairytale I - Growing Pains take an extra to save yourself time later.

Rogue's Purse

[edit | edit source]
Items recommended: Food for low levels to run past aggressive level 53 Jogres, and combat equipment to kill a level 53 Jogre for an easy Diary task

Head north-east of the village towards where you found the Ardrigal. Once you find the end of the cliffs, follow the coastline west until you find a pile of searchable rocks. Search them and enter the cave.

Note: You may kill a Jogre inside the dungeon for an easy Karamja Diary task. Take the jogre bones for Tai Bwo Wannai Trio. Although they may drop the grimy rogue's purse, they do not count for the quest.

Run south past the aggressive level 53 Jogres until you find a fungus covered cavern wall. Search it until you find the grimy rogue's purse (may take some minutes).

Note: If you intend to do Zogre Flesh Eaters, take an extra to save yourself time later.

Return to Trufitus with the final cleaned herb.

Congratulations, quest complete!
