The Knight's Sword is a free-to-play intermediate quest in which the White Knight Squire Asrol needs help making Sir Vyvin a new ceremonial sword.

Walkthrough

[edit | edit source]

Sword trouble

[edit | edit source]
The location of Sir Vyvin's squire.
Items recommended: Fast travel method to Varrock and Mudskipper Point

Talk to the Squire, found in the courtyard of the White Knights' Castle in Falador. He has lost Sir Vyvin's sword, a family heirloom made many generations ago by the Imcando dwarves. Offer to make a new one and he will mention that Reldo the Varrock Palace librarian may know where to find an Imcando dwarf.

Note: If you have already completed The Giant Dwarf, skip the following step and go straight to Thurgo; otherwise you must talk to Reldo before you may continue.

Talk to Reldo in the Varrock Palace library, the northernmost room on the ground floor. Ask him what he knows about Imcando dwarves, and he will point you towards Mudskipper Point, just south of Port Sarim.

Note: If you still need ingredients for the redberry pie required in the following section, you can take the pie dish from the kitchen to the east now, then pick redberries by the South-east Varrock mine.
The location of Thurgo, an Imcando dwarf.

Thurgo the Imcando dwarf

[edit | edit source]
Items needed: Redberry pie
Items recommended: Fast travel method to Falador

Head south of Port Sarim to find Thurgo on the west side of a hill, in his hut with the anvil. Ask him if he would like some redberry pie, and he will take some and eat it. Ask if he can make a special sword, and he requests that you bring him a picture of the lost sword as a reference.

Acquiring the picture

[edit | edit source]

Return to the Squire in the Falador Castle courtyard again, and he will mention that a portrait of Sir Vyvin's father includes the sword.

Note: You must have spoken to the squire before you may obtain the portrait.

From the courtyard of the White Knights' Castle in Falador, go east through three doors, and then up the ladder. Go west through the door and then further west through the open doorway, then up the stairs. Sir Vyvin's room will be directly to the south.

You need to obtain the portrait by searching the cupboard inside his room, but Sir Vyvin will stop you if he is inside the room and looking towards the player. Open the door to his room and wait for him to walk out, or hop worlds until you find one where he is outside the room, then take the portrait from the cupboard.

Mining the blurite

[edit | edit source]
Items needed: A pickaxe, the portrait, 2 iron bars (4 if making extra sword)
Items recommended: Food and plate armour (slash resistant) for low levels, fast travel method to Mudskipper Point and Falador
Skills needed: 10 to mine blurite ore

Return to Thurgo and give him the portrait. He now knows a blurite sword is what he needs to make, and you must obtain the blurite ore for it. This ore is only found in the Asgarnia Ice Dungeon, located on the hill to the north east. All enemies in the dungeon are aggressive, so get ready to run past them. Head down the ladder on the hill, run north through the dungeon until you reach the pirates, then head east down the first corridor and enter the large icy room, which contains several bright blue rocks to mine for the ore. At the south-eastern end of the icy room, there is an entrance to the skeletal wyverns section of the cave. Standing directly in front of this entrance is a safespot, which you may wait in until it is safe to mine the nearby rocks.

Note: Mine 1-2 extra if you are planning to complete the easy Falador Diary and/or want your own blurite sword.

Creating the sword

[edit | edit source]
Items needed: 2 iron bars (4 if making extra sword), blurite ore (2 if making extra sword)
Items recommended: an additional redberry pie if doing The Giant Dwarf

Return to Thurgo with your blurite ores and your iron bars and he will make you a sword. If you want a sword for yourself, drop it and ask Thurgo for a replacement, then pick up the second sword again.

Note: Members planning on doing The Giant Dwarf soon after this quest are recommended to give Thurgo an additional redberry pie now to avoid a return trip during that quest.

Go back to the Squire and he will take one of your blurite swords.

Congratulations, quest complete!

Trivia

[edit | edit source]
  • When trying to complete the quest while having the blurite sword equipped, the Squire would say "So can you un-equip it and hand it over to me now please?"
  • The Smithing experience granted by this quest is so much that, if the player's Smithing level is 1, it will be levelled up to 29 after this quest. If they start at 15 Smithing (to create the iron bars), it will be levelled up to 31.
  • This is the only free-to-play quest that requires a skill above level 1.
