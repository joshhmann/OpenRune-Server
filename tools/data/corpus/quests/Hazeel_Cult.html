Hazeel Cult is a novice quest that was released on 15 August 2002. It was the second quest to feature a Mahjarrat, and introduces Hazeel, a Zamorakian Mahjarrat that has been put into a state of torpor, and his followers are attempting to revive him in time for the next Ritual of Rejuvenation.

This quest is notable in that it has two sides, a "good" side and an "evil" side, sharing this trait with Temple of Ikov, another Mahjarrat quest. In the "good" side, the player foils the cultists in their attempt to resurrect Hazeel, and in the "evil" side, the player joins the cult and assists them in resurrecting Hazeel.

Walkthrough

[edit | edit source]

Hazeel Thieves

[edit | edit source]

Head to the house in the south-western corner of East Ardougne. Speak to Ceril Carnillean, who will start ranting about thieving cultists. Ask him what's wrong; some cultists have stolen a suit of armour, and the player will offer to help get it back. He points you in the direction of a cave to the south of the city. Head east of the clock tower, and enter the cave (south of Valve 3 on the side).

Picking a side

[edit | edit source]

At this stage you will have a choice between two sides in this quest, helping Ceril Carnillean get his family armour back and foiling the plan to revive Hazeel, or helping Hazeel's followers murder a Carnillean and resurrect their master.

The reward is almost the same for both, though siding with Ceril will reward 5 additional coins and gives you the option to obtain Carnillean armour, although this armour has lower defensive bonuses than an iron platebody. Siding with Hazeel will reward the Hazeel's mark, which is a purely cosmetic reward. Rewards may only be obtained during or upon completion of the quest, and can never be reclaimed afterward.

Ceril's side is quicker, but requires killing Alomone, which is not recommended for very low-level players. Your choice will also have a minor effect on the events of the quest Secrets of the North.

If siding with Hazeel, skip the following section and read the next one.

Siding with Ceril

[edit | edit source]
Items needed: Weapon, armour and food

If you've chosen to side with Ceril, talk to Clivet in the cave to learn about Hazeel, an ancient and powerful follower of Zamorak who was killed by an ancestor of Ceril. He will ask for your help in reviving Hazeel. Refuse.

Clivet will sail away on a raft, saying that you'll never be able to find him. Go back upstairs, find the sewer valves, and turn them in the proper direction.

  • Valve 1 - This is right across the bridge south of the Carnilleans' house. Turn it right.
  • Valve 2 - This is right behind Carnilleans' house. Turn it right.
  • Valve 3 - This is north of the cave. Turn it left.
  • Valve 4 - This is south of the zoo's penguin cage. Turn it right.
  • Valve 5 - Go south-east of the fourth valve, past the monk. Turn it right.

After you've turned all five, go back into the cave. Board the raft, then head to the southernmost room and talk to Alomone. He will let it slip that Ceril's butler is part of the cult, before attacking you. Kill him, then open the chest in the room to get the Carnillean armour.

Note: You can use the drop trick if you want your own armour.

Board the raft again in order to return to the sewer entrance. Back at the Carnilleans' house, talk to Ceril Carnillean, who will take the armour from you and give you 5 coins. After the player accuses the butler, he will refuse to believe you.

You'll see a quest reward screen, but you haven't received the real reward yet!

Congratulations! Quest complete!
Or not...

Search the cupboard on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] and you will find the Mark of Hazeel and a bottle of poison. Show these to Ceril. He will fire Butler Jones and give you the real quest reward.

Siding with Hazeel

[edit | edit source]

If you've chosen to side with Hazeel, make sure you have a free inventory slot and then talk to Clivet to learn about Hazeel. He will explain that Hazeel is an ancient and powerful follower of Zamorak who was killed by an ancestor of Ceril. He will ask for your help in reviving Hazeel. Accept.

He wants you to prove your loyalty by killing Ceril. Ask him how, and he will give you some poison and instruct you to pour it in Ceril's food.

Head to the basement of Ceril's house, and use the bottle of poison on the range. Then, go upstairs and talk to Ceril Carnillean to learn that you've accidentally killed Scruffy the family dog instead; his son gave the meal to him.

Talk to Clivet again, and he will have heard about the accident, but takes it as proof of your loyalty regardless. He'll give you Hazeel's mark, then tell you to meet with their leader Alomone in their hideout. To get to the hideout, you need to find five Sewer valves and turn them the proper direction.

  • Valve 1 - This is right across the bridge south of the Carnilleans' house. Turn it right.
  • Valve 2 - This is right behind Carnilleans' house. Turn it right.
  • Valve 3 - This is north of the cave. Turn it left.
  • Valve 4 - This is south of the zoo's penguin cage. Turn it right.
  • Valve 5 - Go south-east of the fourth valve, past the monk. Turn it right.

Go back to the cave and board the raft. Once you're in the main hideout, talk to Alomone. He wants you to help the Carnillean's butler, an undercover cultist, retrieve a magical scroll of restoration that is hidden in the Carnilleans' house.

Talk to Butler Jones in the house, who believes the scroll is hidden where even the family won't find it. Search the crate in the basement for a chest key (make sure to finish the dialogue to get the key in your inventory). Go up the ladder, then up the stairs and knock on the wall in the east room, on the northern side, then finally go up the ladder. Use the key on the chest to get the scroll. Return to Alomone to start Hazeel's resurrection.

Rewards

[edit | edit source]
  • 1 Quest point
  • 1500 Thieving experience
  • 2000 coins (plus 5 from the fake ending if you sided with the Carnillean family)
  • Hazeel's mark (if you sided with Hazeel)
  • Carnillean armour (if you sided with Ceril and obtained an extra during the quest)
  • 5 Kudos, which can be claimed by talking to Historian Minas in the Varrock Museum
