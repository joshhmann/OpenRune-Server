Section 1.4

Various skilling, agility for graceful

Train various skills and complete the graceful outfit grind for unlimited run energy restoration.

52 steps in this section
Section Progress0 / 48 (0%)

Thieve master farmers for a couple of hours just to get some low level seeds to start farming with, mainly harralanders

📍 Draynor
Remember rogue outfit. I recommend thieving in Draynor since the bank is right next to the farmer to grab some food quickly. There is a anti-bot feature for master farmers where if you have less than 71 farming, you get less herb seeds, however according to mod Ash it should only affect ranarr, snaps and torstols. Those seeds aren't important right now so as long as you can get other seeds in good amounts, that's all that matters
Mod Ash tweet about master farmer changes

Get 100% hosidius favour, should have 300 compost in bank

📍 Hosidius
At 45% do mess hall until 100%

Buy pineapples for supercompost from charter ships

📍 Various

Put 15 pineapples into each of the compost bins at the allotment patches, remember to close the bin

📍 Various
Don't do Hosidius patch yet, we'll be doing construction soon which requires house in Rimmington so it's not worth switching house to Kourend for 1 compost run and then switching back

Buy ~200 chocolate from the grand tree food shop, don't buy too much since it's faster later from the RFD chest

📍 Gnome Stronghold

Make energy pots, should have some harralanders from kingdom if it's been over a day since you did the quest

📍 Various

Camelot tele

📍 Seers Village

Plant barley seeds at the hops patch northwest of Seers, pay the farmer 3 normal compost to look after them

📍 Seers Village

Minigame tele to blast furnace

📍 Keldagrim

Do the Giant Dwarf quest

📍 Keldagrim📜 The Giant Dwarf ✓
If you need more rubies or emeralds, the gem stall in keldagrim is usually stocked

These next 2 quests have times where you have to wait for stuff to happen, smith iron dart tips while you wait

📍 Keldagrim

Forgettable tale of a drunken dwarf quest

📍 Keldagrim📜 Forgettable Tale... ✓
There's an anvil near the bank in keldagrim. Your barley will be ready when you need it, use it on a range to get the barley malt. Plant jute seeds after harvesting the barley for a diary task, don't need to compost or protect them. Do not waste the 2 dwarven stout(m) that you get as the reward!

Garden of tranquility quest

📍 Varrock📜 Garden of Tranquility ✓

Start Enlightened journey quest

📍 Entrana📜 Enlightened Journey
You need 6 willow branches for the falador diary on top of the 12 needed for the quest so get those as well

While waiting for the willow trees to grow, do these steps:

📍 Various

Do Eyes of glouphrie and Tower of life

📍 Gnome Stronghold📜 The Eyes of Glouphrie ✓
Bring a raw chicken and a raw swordfish to kill a Swordchick for Ardy diary after Tower of life

Smith the rest of your iron dart tips, leave 20 bars for quests

📍 Various

Mine Volcanic ash for ultracompost, get like 2k so you don't have to constantly come back to mine more

📍 Fossil Island

Do a farm run of limpwurts, harralanders and ultracompost

📍 Various
Store 15 supercompost in leprechaun for a quest, make the rest into ultras. Use volcanic ash on the full bin rather than individual buckets of supercompost. I'm not gonna be mentioning farm runs anymore, just do them whenever. Always use ultra compost on herbs, for limps i'd just use normal compost because the seeds are so easy to get

Do Temple of Ikov quest

📍 McGrubor's Wood📜 Temple of Ikov ✓
Start fletching iron darts while you run around doing quests. If you haven't got a yew bow from medium clues that you may have got from skilling, get a yew long from young implings in puro-puro, find a spawn point and catch them as they spawn

Finish Enlightened journey and unlock Varrock and Castle wars balloons

📍 Entrana📜 Enlightened Journey ✓
While unlikely, it's possible that you didn't get yew logs from wintertodt to unlock castle wars, in that case you'll just have to run between Varrock east bank and the sawmill instead of doing the method explained just below

Construction method: Make 10+ dueling rings, equip whichever axe you got from tree spirits earlier

📍 Various

Start at Castle wars, cut 65 willow logs and deposit them to the log storage next to the balloon

📍 Castle Wars

Balloon to Varrock, cut full inventory of oak logs and turn them into planks at the sawmill

📍 Varrock
3-ticking recommended especially if u didn't get a lucky rune axe
3 Oaks just next to the balloon

Teleport to Castle wars, repeat until you have 50 construction banked

📍 Castle Wars
You can use minigame tele every 20mins if you want to save a ring charge. 1500 planks not including Wintertodt XP, check xp calculator if you want to get the exact amount

Get 50 construction, un-note with Phials at Rimmington general store

📍 Rimmington🎯 50 construction
lvl 22-33, oak dining table in the dining room. lvl 33-50, oak larder in the kitchen

Move your house to kourend now for farm patch

📍 Great Kourend

Start doing daily tree runs from now on with the seeds you get from birdhouses and later farming contracts as well

📍 Various

Do these quests while fletching iron darts: Big chompy bird hunt, Rag and bone man (first part, should have all bones in bank already), Family crest (get goldsmith gauntlets after quest is done), Start Legend's quest (Get vanilla pod for RFD), Death plateau, Troll Stronghold

📍 Various📜 Troll Stronghold ✓

I'll assume you have fletched all of them now, if you haven't then finish the rest of them during next step underwater

📍 Various

Go to the small island at fossil island with the bank, where you dive to seaweed patches

📍 Fossil Island

While underwater: Fletch maple shortbows (u) until 55 fletching

📍 Fossil Island🎯 55 fletching
Make sure you fletch all of the darts first. The goal here is to pick up the seaweed spores that spawn every now and then. The spores can spawn north of the anchor so make sure you're checking the minimap and not just your item overlay. Plant seaweed after you get your first 2 spores. You will get a weight warning but you can just ignore that, it makes no difference unless you go to the deep water

Fletch all of your maple logs into maple longbows (u)

📍 Fossil Island
Make sure to leave some logs for birdhouses

Cut all of your uncut gems

📍 Fossil Island

If u have any molten glass left, blow all of it into unpowered orbs, make 2 lantern lenses as well

📍 Fossil Island

Do seaweed farming every time u do birdhouse run from now on, very important!

📍 Fossil Island

Buy 2800 gold ore from blast furnace ore shop, 1 inv per world

📍 Keldagrim
Don't use energy pots for this or the actual smithing part, only run with empty inventory

Get 60 smithing, remember to wear goldsmith gauntlets

📍 Keldagrim🎯 60 smithing

Go to Edgeville

📍 Edgeville

Craft the gold bars into gold BRACELETS, leave like 30 bars for teleport jewellery if you need to make more

📍 Edgeville
Reminder to buy rubies from either keldagrim or kourend castle gem stalls if you run out. The reason we are doing bracelets instead of amulets is the extra 120gp per high alch, which is worth more than 5xp

If you have 60 magic, get mage arena cape while you're here anyway

📍 Mage Bank

Buy another 5k nats, get some cosmics too if you need more

📍 Mage Bank
⚠️ HCIM: HCIM; if scared, you should still have enough nats to get 63mage with alching, and then you can boost to get into wizard guild

Do agility until full graceful, 50-60 at Canifis, 60+ at Seers

📍 Canifis/Seers🎯 60 agility
Alch your gold bracelets and maple bows(u) while doing agility. Loot kingdom and fletch more longbows to alch if you run out. If you're still getting more maples from kingdom then it's not worth doing agility if u run out of alchs, just move to the next step and come back to agility few days later. Make sure you keep at least 500 logs for birdhouses!

If you want a break from agility, fish 3k raw karambwans. Banking at Zanaris bank is your best option for now

📍 Karamja

Should get to 70+ mage by the time you're done with agility for now

📍 Various🎯 70 magic

Kingdom reminder! If u haven't already, take out all gp from the coffers after the 7th day, total 6.2k maples

📍 Miscellania

Do the Regicide quest

📍 Isafdar📜 Regicide ✓
Pick up a few whiteberries just north of the elf tracker

Do Hand in the sand quest

📍 Yanille📜 Hand in the Sand ✓
