Ironman Money Making Guide

Every GP/hr figure uses actual coins — High Alchemy values, direct drops, and NPC shop sales. Not GE prices. Updated for 2026 with Sailing methods.

The Ironman Alch Pipeline

Ironmen cannot use the Grand Exchange. Every coin in your cash stack comes from one of three sources: High Alchemy (converting items to coins at 60% of base value), direct coin drops from monsters and thieving, or NPC shop sales.

All GP/hr figures on this page are actual coins, not GE values. This is the only ironman money guide that uses correct ironman economics.

Daily Money Loop

Passive methods you should do regularly. These take minutes per day but add up to millions over time.

Battlestaves (Zaff Daily)

Buy 15-120 staves from Zaff depending on diary tier. Craft into elemental battlestaves and alch for 2,300gp profit each. Hard diary (60/day) = 138k/day. Elite (120/day) = 276k/day.

Birdhouse Runs

Check and replace birdhouses on Fossil Island. Nests give seed and ring rewards. Alch duplicate rings and sell seeds. Main value is seeds for farming, but rings provide some direct GP.

Herb Runs

Plant and harvest herbs at 7-9 patches. Ranarr herbs sell to shops for ~7,500gp each. Most ironmen keep herbs for potions, but you CAN sell to shops if coins are the bottleneck.

50k-150k (if selling herbs to shops)Every 80 minutes

Kingdom of Miscellania

Maintain 100% approval and 750k+ in the coffer. Costs 75k/day but returns herbs, seeds, coal, and resources worth significantly more. Not direct money — but essential for supply generation.

Costs 75k/day (returns ~2-3x in supplies)Daily (check weekly)

Farming Contracts

Complete farming contracts at the Farming Guild for seed packs. High-level contracts give tree and herb seeds. Duplicate seeds can be sold to the seed stall NPC for coins.

30k-80k (from seed nest alchables)Variable (per contract)
45+ FarmingFarming Guild access
Selling Cats to West Ardougne

Raise a kitten into a cat, then sell to the West Ardougne civilian for 200 death runes. At ~255gp per death rune, each cat is worth ~51,000gp. Fully passive — just keep a kitten following you.

200 death runes (51,000gp value)Every 3 hours (per cat)
Gertrude's Cat quest

GP Sinks

What ironmen actually spend money on. Understanding your expenses helps you plan which money methods to prioritize.

Construction

29-140M

The biggest ironman money sink. Planks from the sawmill (1-1.5k each) add up fast. 83 Construction for max house costs ~29M. Full 99 costs ~140M. Most ironmen aim for 83-85.

mid

Kingdom of Miscellania

27M/year

75,000gp/day to maintain at full approval (10% of 750k coffer per day). Returns herbs, seeds, coal, logs, and other supplies worth significantly more than the cost. Essential passive.

mid

Blast Furnace (Smithing)

30-54M

72,000gp/hr to use the official BF worlds, plus ore costs. Smithing gold bars for 99 costs ~30M in ore. Runite bars for alchs require coal (free from Kingdom) and runite ore (mined or PvM).

mid

Sailing (Ship Upgrades)

20-30M

Ship hull upgrades, sail upgrades, crew hiring, and voyage supplies. Higher-tier ships require significant coin investment but unlock better content and rewards.

mid

Boss Instances

200k-500k/session

Private instances at GWD (200k), DKs (25k), and other bosses. GWD instances are essentially required for ironmen to avoid competition. Adds up over hundreds of hours of PvM.

late

PvM Death Fees

100k-500k per death

Dying at bosses costs 100k-500k to reclaim gear from Death's Office. Learning new bosses means many deaths. Death's Coffer credit helps offset this. Budget 5-10M for learning each new boss.

late

Rune Purchases (Shops)

5-15M total

Buying runes from shops for burst/barrage Slayer, charging trident, and general magic use. Nature runes (~200gp each), cosmic runes for orbs, and blood runes add up.

mid

NPC Services & Misc

2-5M total

Includes charter ships (~3,600gp each for Crafting training), NPC unnoting fees, tanning leather, imbuing items at NMZ. Small individually but adds up.

early
