Section 1.5

Diaries and finishing RFD

Complete achievement diaries and finish Recipe for Disaster for Barrows gloves - the best-in-slot gloves for most of the game.

20 steps in this section
Section Progress0 / 17 (0%)

Do all of the RFD subquests

📍 Various📜 Recipe for Disaster
If u didn't fish the 3k karambwans during agility for cook xp, u can leave awowogei's quest until later

Buy a bunch of chocolate dust from the chest and make energy pots

📍 Lumbridge

Plant some Toadflax seeds, agility potion is needed soon for a quest

📍 Various
If you don't have toadflax seed for some reason, you can do brimhaven agility arena and get 2 toadflax from there

Make a mithril grapple

📍 Various

Do these quests for QP and diaries: A soul's bane, Another slice of H.A.M, Icthlarin's little helper, In search of the Myreque, In aid of the Myreque, Eagles' peak, Enakhra's Lament, Rat catchers, Olaf's quest, Between a rock, Tai bwo wannai trio, Zogre flesh eater

📍 Various

Rum deal (Req 42 slayer, you should be close to it. Get a task from Mazchna for Mory diary if you don't have one)

📍 Braindeath Island📜 Rum Deal ✓
For the 47 prayer req, you can use the ancient mace spec to boost

Cabin fever

📍 Mos Le'Harmless📜 Cabin Fever ✓

One small favour

📍 Various📜 One Small Favour ✓

Watchtower quest

📍 Yanille📜 Watchtower ✓

Do all easy and medium diaries

📍 Various
Kourend diaries can be left undone, I dont do them until I have Xerics tally from shamans and staminas for the quests. Wildy and fremmy mediums require higher slayer, leave those for when you go back to slayer. You probably don't have a crystal key for fally medium diary yet so leave that for later as well

For Lumby 50 range req I'd recommend ranging at pest control with bone cbow

📍 Pest Control🎯 50 ranged
If you're not 70cb for intermediate boat then go do slayer now, train attack to 60 then defense until 70cb. May as well stay until you get 250pts for void bottoms, good mage def. Need 55 range eventually anyway for red chins

Do Slayer until 60 attack

📍 Various🎯 60 attack

Buy Dragon scim

📍 Ape Atoll
Normally I have recommended to also buy a Dragon battleaxe, but it's really just not needed anymore due to how easy it is to get kwuarms and limpwurts nowadays with farming contracts

Do Eadgar's ruse

📍 Trollheim📜 Eadgar's Ruse ✓

Do My arm's big adventure

📍 Trollheim📜 My Arm's Big Adventure ✓

Do Desert Treasure

📍 Various📜 Desert Treasure ✓
If you didn't get magic logs from Wintertodt, go catch nature implings in puro-puro, you should have 58 hunter easily by now from birdhouses

Finish RFD, buy B gloves

📍 Lumbridge📜 Recipe for Disaster ✓
