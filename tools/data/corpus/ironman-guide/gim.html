GIM Team Strategies

Role Splitting Guide

Divide responsibilities among team members to maximize efficiency. Each member focuses on specific skills that benefit the whole group.

Resource Gatherer

  • - Mining, Smithing, Crafting focus
  • - Provides gear and supplies to group
  • - Blast Furnace specialist
  • - Gem mining for jewelry

PvM Specialist

  • - Combat and Slayer focus
  • - Boss farming for uniques
  • - Provides drops to group
  • - Raids when ready

Skiller Support

  • - Herblore and Farming focus
  • - Provides potions for PvM
  • - Manages herb runs for group
  • - Cooking and food prep

Quest/Diary Specialist

  • - Prioritizes quest unlocks
  • - Achievement diary completionist
  • - Unlocks teleports and areas
  • - Fairy rings, spirit trees early

Shared Storage Optimization

  • 1.
    Prioritize Shared Items - Store items that benefit multiple members: teleport jewelry, runes, potions, and food.
  • 2.
    Coordinate Deposits - Communicate before depositing large stacks. 80 slots fills up fast with poor planning.
  • 3.
    Use for Trading - Drop trades are allowed, but shared storage is safer and faster for transferring items.
  • 4.
    Keep Supplies Stocked - Designate someone to keep food, potions, and commonly needed items always available.

Team Coordination Tips

Communication

  • - Use Discord or group chat for coordination
  • - Share progress updates regularly
  • - Call out valuable drops for group decisions
  • - Plan boss trips together for efficiency

Early Game Coordination

  • - One person rushes quests, others skill
  • - Share early teleport jewelry
  • - Coordinate Wintertodt as a group activity
  • - Split up achievement diaries for efficiency

Mid/Late Game

  • - Team bossing for faster kills
  • - Share rare drops fairly (or based on need)
  • - Coordinate Raids learner runs
  • - Help carry newer players through content

GIM-Specific Features

Prestige System

Groups start with full prestige. Leaving or adding members affects prestige level and hiscores visibility. Consider this before making group changes.

Hardcore Group Ironman

Optional hardcore mode available. Group loses hardcore status after a set number of deaths based on group size. Extra challenging!

Group Hiscores

Track your group's total level and compete against other GIM groups. Coordinate leveling for hiscores rankings.
