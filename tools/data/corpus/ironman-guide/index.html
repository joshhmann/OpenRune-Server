Ironman Efficiency Guide

By OzirisLoL • Version 4.0 • 569 steps

Based on the original Oziris efficiency guide, updated for 2026 with Sailing, Varlamore, Royal Titans, and Yama. Toggle Enhanced Mode for modern updates or read the classic guide as-is.

Before You Start

  • • Use every book and lamp on Herblore until 77, then Prayer (HCIM) or Agility/RC
  • • Use Authenticator and 2-step verification on your registered email
  • • There is no single right way to play - feel free to do your own thing
  • • This guide focuses on efficiency, but enjoy the journey!
