Do Horror from the deep quest

📍 Lighthouse📜 Horror from the Deep ✓
Complete a lap of Barb agility course for a diary. Just mage the dagannoth and hide when it's weak to melee and range. Turn your run off so you don't get meleed. Use proper food like swordfish and karambwans

Do Turael slayer with dorg cbow until 37 Range and 18 slayer

📍 Burthorpe🎯 18 slayer
Use Turael, every 10th task should be done with the highest level slayer master you can use. Even though we are using Turael, the goal is to get slayer xp so you should always kill monsters with higher hp, for example on bird tasks kill terrorbirds. Vannaka has too many bad tasks so I think this is overall better

Kill tree spirits with fire strike for a mithril axe AND either another mith axe, addy axe or a rune axe, whichever is first

📍 Enchanted Valley
The 2nd axe is for actual WC training, not worth the time camping for rune one unless u get lucky since u can just buy one at 60wc

Do the Underground pass quest

📍 Underground Pass📜 Underground Pass ✓
⚠️ HCIM: Use the mithril armour for this quest. HCIM; While this quest might seem like a dangerous one without overheads, it's honestly not. You can trap the paladins behind each other same way as the captain from the tourist trap quest. For the spider, there are 2 skeletons on the east wall, stand between them and then mage the big one with earth blast. Sometimes the little ones attack you but just run a bit north and then back to the same spot, they'll get stuck
Video of completing the quest with same combat stats

📍 Edgeville

Kill blue dragons with iban's blast for 43 prayer

📍 Taverley Dungeon🎯 43 prayer
We don't have the agility level for shortcut yet so don't bother banking the hides, not worth doing twice as many trips. Add 2400 xp to your current prayer xp and calculate how many bones you need, level 43 requires 50339xp and 1 bone is 288xp with ectofuntus (2.4k pray xp is from ghosts ahoy quest which will be completed before using the bones). Regular ironmen can use the wildy altar if you want to, but either way it's only around 85 bones with ecto so do whichever method. Get at least 1 extra dragon bone on top of 43 prayer, required for the Watchtower quest

📍 Pollnivneach

Get 88 thieving with blackjacking, should get you about 1.4m GP

📍 Pollnivneach🎯 88 thieving
Again, LMS can be done instead if you really hate blackjacking. If u want something to AFK during this grind, i'd heavily recommend ammonite crabs to get 60 attack and then focus on either strength with D Scim or range with bone cbow. If you really like blackjacking then feel free to get higher than 88, the extra money should be used for keeping kingdom running for longer, switch to 10herbs and 5maples in that case

REMINDER to take out GP from kingdom after the 7th day. This thieving grind could take some people 1 day and for others multiple days so it's up to you to keep track of it. You should have 6.2k maples from kingdom when you stop using it
