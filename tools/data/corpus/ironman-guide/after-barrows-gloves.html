Section 2.0

Goals after Barrows gloves

End-game progression goals including Song of the Elves, Dragon Slayer 2, and Corrupted Gauntlet.

66 steps in this section
Section Progress0 / 41 (0%)

Get autoweed perk from Tithe farm (if 54 farming), herb sack IMO isn't even worth getting nowadays with everything giving more herbs and seeds than you'd ever need

📍 Hosidius

Start doing farming contracts as soon as you can do medium contracts, very important!! You can use garden pies to get mediums at 62farm, and hards at 82. Pies can be bought from Cooking guild

📍 Farming Guild

If you ever need something AFK to do, just go to ammonite crabs with either bone cbow if you want to catch less chins in the future, or melee them to speed up slayer

📍 Various

Get 61 Crafting

📍 Various🎯 61 crafting

Go to the desert quarry and mine sandstone for 1.2k buckets of sand

📍 Desert Quarry
Walk from the rocks to the grinder to maintain run energy if you're 3-ticking

Go to rogues' den and burn giant seaweed on the fire, 4 seaweed at a time

📍 Rogues' Den

Make molten glass at Edgeville furnace

📍 Edgeville

Blow glass lenses, might as well do it underwater again for more seaweed spores

📍 Fossil Island

Get 70 mining and do Lunar's diplomacy

📍 Various📜 Lunar Diplomacy ✓🎯 70 mining
Only 60 mining is needed for now so you can do this in bits if you want to, but i'd recommend 70 for making friends with my arm (Req 72mining but u can boost with dwarven stout(m) which u should have in bank) and to complete fremmy hard diary very early for the herb patch shortcut. In my opinion the best way to do this is to fill the sandstone grinder with 25k buckets worth of sand to have it ready for later, bring 8 waterskins, drop them as they empty for inv space and then do a birdhouse run. Finish 70 with your favorite mining method

Get a helm of neitiznot

📍 Rellekka

Get a fighter torso from BA

📍 Barbarian Assault

Strength amulet is as good as a glory ammy for melee slayer (sometimes even better) so just use that for now

📍 Various

Do slayer until 65 attack and 65 str, if you don't have them already

📍 Various🎯 65 strength

Get a dragon defender

📍 Warriors Guild
Train defense to 60 while getting defenders

Prioritize STR training during slayer, I like to go for 65/80/65 melee stats and from there just train each skill 5 levels before switching to the next

📍 Various

Get 58 slayer, make sure you're consistent with all your farming and birdhouses

📍 Various🎯 58 slayer
Personally I like to do Turael point boosting until around 150 tasks, every 10th task from Konar just to get all the good unlocks and blocklist sorted before doing normal slayer, but you can do slayer however you like

I recommend doing hard clues, they're good gp/h at this point of the account. Train crafting for blue dhide if you get those steps using the superglass make lunar spell

📍 Various

Prioritize slayer points on: Superior slayer > Blocklist > Extend your favorite tasks > Slay ring > Slay helm > Garg smasher

📍 Various

At 58 slayer: Camp Cave horrors for mask

📍 Mos Le'Harmless

If you have around 65/80/65 melee stats now, do Making friends with my arm quest and make both of the basalt tele portals in your POH

📍 Weiss📜 Making Friends with My Arm ✓
The quest can be done with lower stats, but the boss can be annoying if you're not hitting well

Do Fremmy hard diary, get 70 agility and make few agility pots to use the shortcut to speed up your herb runs even more, drink the boost before using the teleport to skip the shortcut

📍 Rellekka🎯 70 agility

Do dream mentor and imbue your mask

📍 Lunar Isle📜 Dream Mentor ✓

Get crafting level for slayer rings at some point

📍 Various

Keep doing slayer until level 75

📍 Various🎯 75 slayer
If you want to boost brews from ToB for Song of the elves you can stop at around 70slay, whenever u have 70herb banked. You can go for a B ring during a dagannoth task if you want, 70def with full rune is decent for camping Rex

Do Mage arena 2

📍 Mage Arena

Get 77 herblore, you should have it banked by now if you've been farming, if you don't then just keep doing more slayer

📍 Various🎯 77 herblore
You can finish all the quests that give herb xp or lamps, some hard diaries can also be done

Get 70 agility if you didn't get it for the herb patch shortcut earlier

📍 Various

Cut and bank 2.2k teak logs at fossil island, from the farming patch teaks

📍 Fossil Island

Do Mahogany homes with teak planks for 70 construction

📍 Various🎯 70 construction

Finish 70 woodcutting with any method

📍 Various🎯 70 woodcutting

Get 60 range with bone crossbow at pest control if you haven't already got 60

📍 Pest Control🎯 60 ranged

Get 70 smithing

📍 Keldagrim🎯 70 smithing
If you can't afford to do it all with gold, you can buy like 2k coal and 2k iron and make steel platebodies. Steel is a bit slower but you profit GP from it so it's still a decent method. If you don't care about rushing DS2 and Sote, just go do more slayer until you can afford to do 70 smithing with gold only

Make botanical pies and +4 herblore boost for sara brews

📍 Various

Do Song of the elves

📍 Prifddinas📜 Song of the Elves ✓
If you don't have 80 magic yet for blitz, you could go do MTA for B2P, master wand and inf boots. Use blood blitz against Seren. If you go in with inventory full of brews and restores, it's basically impossible to fail the fight

Hunt 15k red chins at the Prif hunter area

📍 Prifddinas

Get range void from PC

📍 Pest Control

Unlock MM2 chinning area and chin to at least 87 range

📍 Ape Atoll🎯 87 ranged
At 70 range, if you're 100cb do western hard diary and upgrade to elite void

Do Dragon slayer 2

📍 Varrock📜 Dragon Slayer II ✓

Start using kingdom again now that you'll be getting constant GP easily, put it on 10 teaks or mahogany, whichever you prefer to use for construction and 5 on herbs

📍 Miscellania

Ending the guide here, you can pretty much do whatever you want from here

📍 Various

By far the best thing to do next is to camp Corrupted Gauntlet until 2 enhanced weapon seeds to make both of the weapons and 6 armor seeds for full crystal

📍 Prifddinas
