📍 Wintertodt
UPDATE (Oct 2024): The Warmth Meter system has replaced HP-based damage entirely. A 10 HP player and a 99 HP player now take identical warmth damage, so there is no longer any advantage to rushing Wintertodt at low HP. Wear 4 warm clothing items for optimal warmth (Clue Hunter boots/gloves + Rainbow jumper/scarf from Gilbert in Lumbridge). Rejuvenation potions now restore 30% Warmth per dose so no external food is needed. Supply crates have been replaced by a Reward Cart with a Big-search option. Bruma Herbs no longer grant Farming XP. Do whatever method you want; solo, mass, fletching the logs. If you want, feel free to get 99 firemaking in one go
