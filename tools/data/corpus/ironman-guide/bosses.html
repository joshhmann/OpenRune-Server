Notes on boss order

  • This order is a recommendation, not a strict requirement. Adjust based on your account and goals.
  • Slayer bosses (purple) are done passively during Slayer tasks when you reach the required level.
  • DT2 bosses can be done in any order, but Vardorvis first is recommended for the Ultor Ring and Soulreaper Axe.
  • Raids (CoX, ToB, ToA) order depends on your team and what drops you need most.
  • Rigour and Augury from CoX replace Deadeye and Mystic Vigour from Royal Titans.
